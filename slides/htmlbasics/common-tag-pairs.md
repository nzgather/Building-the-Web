## Common tag pairs

Heading tags:

	<h1>Biggest heading</h1>
	<h3>Medium heading</h3>
	<h6>Smallest heading</h6>

Normal text tags:

	<p>Paragraph of text</p>
	<strong>Strong, heavy text in bold</strong>
	<em>Emphasised text in italics</em>
